The   Green Guide Â© is the global standard for the assessment of safe capacity at sports grounds.


First published and owned by the SGSA in 1973, the current 6th Edition was published in 2018.


It is the foundation logic of planning a safe capacity and still the starting point of crowd management capacity plans.


At Dynamic Crowd Measurement we like technology platforms coding and automation for ease of use so thought The Green Guide process should be provided as a free app to use on field devices as well as desktop planning exercises.


Note:  using this automated assessment guide does not change any of the rules or requirements as set out in the Green Guide 6th edition, - it just saves you using a spread sheet and provides a easy end summary sheet.
