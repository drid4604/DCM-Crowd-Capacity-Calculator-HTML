This tool provides basic calculations for crowd safety based on guidelines discussed in the Green Guide.

The tool is designed for specific general scenarios, and should only be used by professionals qualified to construct a crowd management plan who can determine suitability of the calculator when modelling any given space.

When using the tool, the specific sections of the Green Guide relevant to each input or calculation  should be considered, as well as the guide in its entirety. This context is vital in determining whether the tool is appropriate for the situation at hand.
